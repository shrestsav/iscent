What we Do Have you ever walked into a favorite store or hotel and noticed a familiar scent? That’s scent marketing. Bespoke aromas that create a consistent, desirable, ambient scent for a brand, place or product. In fact, scent is one of the most powerful, emotion inducing senses we have, and now we are bringing that technology into your home.

Experts in the field, we have created a new diffuser system for your home that gives you access to the endless fragrance choices available for commercial premises, now you can have the scent you want and not be restricted to a few choices. With a library of over 1000 different scents, including many that you know from all the most popular stores and hotels from throughout Dubai, as well as the ability to create completely custom fragrances to match your desires, we are changing the face of home diffusers.

Until now, your home diffuser has been restricted to just a limited number of choices, but we believe that your home should use the aroma you want, and with our catalogue of scents, including many you will be familiar as used in Burj Khalifa, the Westin, Burj Al Arab, The One Home Furniture, Zara Home, Sauce, Abercrombie & Fitch, and many more, you have real choice to have the scent you want. In addition, our expert perfumers can create any scent you want, if you are looking for a particular Tom Ford, or Chanel scent to fragrance your home with, look no further. Our perfumers are here to help create a memorable experience for you and your guests.

How We Do It As with our scents, we believe in choice, that is why we offer two different diffusers for your home.

The first is a standalone fragrance diffuser, this can cover up to 100sqm, and is perfect for those smaller areas or for those rooms and spaces without ducted ventilation systems.

The second option is our HVAC diffuser, a beautifully crafted scent system that sits within your existing ventilation system delivering a truly invisible ambient scent solution. Our highly experienced, trained technicians will visit your home and determine the best installation position for your system, and during installation will even clean or replace your AC filter at no cost to you to ensure everything is working perfectly from the start. These ultra-reliable diffusers only need servicing once every three months as well, and provide long term ambient scent, quietly and invisibly.

Whichever option suits you best, you get the same quality of service and amazing choice of scents, including custom scents created just for you.
