Can I change my scent every month?

For the monthly subscriptions, you can change the fragrance after the completion of each month. For the three-month subscription, you can change the fragrance once during the duration of the term. For the six-month subscription, you can change the fragrance twice during the duration of the term. For the one year subscription, you can change four times.
